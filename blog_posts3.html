WANDERER

Vaibhav Singh

3/02/2020

Microsoft

The wildest trip I've ever taken involved total silence .

We booked the trip last minute, not knowing what we were getting ourselves into- and we had a great time! Sokha was very accommodating. He helped us arrange transport from PP airport, met us at our accommodation for a pre-trip briefing and then we took off on a 3D2N trek. I have done a fair amount of jungle trekking around South East Asia and this was the most primitive and'roughing-it' type of trekking I've done. The fact that it was rainy season probably added to the challenges we faced getting to the trailhead by motorbike as well as the trek through the jungle.
